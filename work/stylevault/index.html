StyleVault is the first project management tool created exclusively for top fashion stylists. Stylists could create a fashion project, take photos of looks, keep track of individual items with receipt photos, and export all of this information into a budget-calculated and formatted PDF â€” right from their phone.

Many of Los Angeles' top stylists signed on to use our product exclusively for their work. Notable stylists include:

  • Johnny Wujek. (Katy Perry, Kate Mara, Amber Heard.)
  • Tara Swennen. (Kristen Stewart, Julie Bowen, Kaley Cuoco.)
  • Ilaria Urbinati. (Bradley Cooper, Nina Dobrev, Krysten Ritter.)

As a co-founder and sole developer, I worked with co-founder Jonathan DeRoche to design the app before personally building the functionality in its entirety. A Parse database and Cloud Code were used as a simple back-end solution for fast iteration and flexibility.
